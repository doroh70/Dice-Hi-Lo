Dice Hi Lo

Description

Dice Hi-Lo is a fun, easy game that can be played with No prior experience, as the outcome is random.
A player will play against the house and bet high or low when they create their game room.

Rules

  • Rule 1: 2 dice are rolled
  • Rule 2: You bet if your next roll will be higher or lower than the house roll
  • Rule 3: If you win, you get the total pot
  • Rule 4: If you lose, you lose your bet
  • Rule 5: Player and house have individual dice rolls (2 six sided die)
  • Rule 6: Each player bets an amount, bets increment by 10
  • Rule 7: The player has a pool of chips they use to bet
  • Rule 8: The player sets the bet amount by using the increase or decrease button to change the bet amount
  • Rule 9: The playerâ€™s chip pool changes based on the win or loss of the bet
  • Rule 10: When the rounds are done the the player or house with the most chips win
  • Rule 11: If the player has all the chips they win if the house has all the chips they win
  • Rule 12: If all rounds are completed, whoever has the most chips, wins
  • Rule 13: Game ends
